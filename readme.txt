Coordinate System:
The Coordinate System for Space has two variables: x and y.
x=up and down
y=left and right
so if you go 7 mKM right, 6 mKM left, your coords start at:
X 0 
Y 0
---
After:
X 7
Y -6

Say Planet A is at X 7 Y -7. If you are 1 X or 1 Y away from a planet, there is a message. "A planet is really close."
The nearest planets are displayed on radar, except being in interstellar, in which the nearest stars are displayed.
